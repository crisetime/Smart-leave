About Leave hive

Leave hive a online portal for our respected teachers to apply their leave online and get response over the internet without going anywhere and filling any kind of pen paper form. We bring every process online such as history of every employee who has taken a leave. and given every user option to check there leave is approved or not and check how many leave they still remaining.
